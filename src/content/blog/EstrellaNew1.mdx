**Zootopia 2 Gets 2025 Release Date as Disney Reveals Timings for Toy Story 5, Frozen 3, and More**

**IGN Staff**

Disney has officially announced the release date for the highly anticipated _Zootopia 2_, set to hit theaters in 2025. This announcement comes as part of a broader reveal of upcoming release dates for other major sequels, including _Toy Story 5_ and _Frozen 3_.

The original _Zootopia_, released in 2016, was a massive hit, grossing over $1 billion worldwide and earning an Academy Award for Best Animated Feature. The sequel promises to bring back beloved characters Judy Hopps and Nick Wilde, along with new adventures in the vibrant city of Zootopia.

In addition to _Zootopia 2_, Disney has slated _Toy Story 5_ and _Frozen 3_ for future releases. _Toy Story 5_ will continue the story of Woody, Buzz, and the rest of the toy gang, while _Frozen 3_ will further explore the magical world of Arendelle and its beloved characters, Elsa and Anna.

These announcements were made during Disney's annual shareholder meeting, where the company also provided updates on other upcoming projects. The release of these sequels is part of Disney's strategy to continue building on the success of its iconic franchises.

Fans of _Zootopia_, _Toy Story_, and _Frozen_ can look forward to more adventures and stories in the coming years, as Disney continues to expand its beloved animated universe.
